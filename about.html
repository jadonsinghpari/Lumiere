Our Story

Born from a Passion for Beauty

LUMIÈRE was founded in 2015 by award-winning designer Ananya Mehta, who believed that exceptional design should not be reserved for a privileged few, but should be accessible to anyone who cherishes beauty in their everyday life.

Over a decade, we have grown from a small studio in Bandra to one of India's most trusted luxury interior design brands, with clients across Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, and beyond.

Every piece in our collection is chosen or designed with extraordinary care — sourced from master craftspeople in Italy, Scandinavia, and India, and curated to meet our exacting standards of quality and beauty.
